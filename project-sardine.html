About the project

What is Sardine Escape?

Sardine Escape is an arcade-style mobile game where players swim through an endless ocean, avoiding obstacles and surviving for as long as possible. Using tilt or touch controls, the goal is simple: go as far as you can and beat your best distance.

As players progress, they can collect coins to unlock a variety of fish skins with different rarities. Through a vending machine system, each “tin” reveals a new surprise, adding a layer of progression and excitement. The game also integrates Game Center for global leaderboards and iCloud sync, ensuring a connected and competitive experience.
