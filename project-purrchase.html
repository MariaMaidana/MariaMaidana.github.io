Purrchase.

Purrchase is a home management application focused on reducing food waste. It allows users to catalog their pantry, track expiration dates with smart notifications, and seamlessly integrate inventory with shopping lists and recipe creation.

About the project

What is Purrchase?

Purrchase was born from the need to transform kitchen management into a simple and visual task. By centralizing your home inventory in one place, the app provides a clear view of what you own and what needs to be consumed first.

The app serves as a vital companion for grocery shopping by bridging the gap between your kitchen and the market. Users can store their favorite recipes within the app, making it easy to check exactly which ingredients are required while shopping.
