Rotta.

Rotta is an iOS app designed to make Formula racing more accessible, providing information about categories like Formula 2, Formula 3, and F1 Academy, along with race calendars, standings, and detailed insights into drivers, teams, and car components.

About the project

What is Rotta?

Rotta was created to simplify how fans follow the development categories of Formula racing, which are often harder to track than Formula 1. By centralizing key information in one place, the app makes it easier to stay updated on races, drivers, and team performance without needing multiple sources.

In addition to competition data, Rotta provides educational content about the sport, including explanations of car components, team structures, and driver profiles. Built with UIKit, the app emphasizes structured navigation, data organization, and performance, using Core Data and CloudKit to manage and sync information efficiently.
