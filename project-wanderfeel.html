WanderFeel.

WanderFeel is a travel journal app that helps users document their journeys while tracking their emotions, combining memories, photos, and personal reflections into a meaningful experience.

About the project

What is Wanderfeel?

↗ GitHub

Unlike traditional travel apps that focus only on locations, WanderFeel centers on how each moment feels. Users can log emotions throughout their journey, turning simple trips into rich, personal stories that evolve over time.

The core of the app is the emotional journey: users can log how they feel throughout their travels, building a deeper connection to their experiences over time. Designed and developed entirely by me using SwiftUI, the app focuses on simplicity, clarity, and a calm visual identity that enhances storytelling.
