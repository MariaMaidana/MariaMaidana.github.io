Cerne.

Built with Apple-native technologies, the app transforms ecological data into an interactive and educational experience.

About the project

What is Cerne?

Cerne is an app designed to make environmental data more accessible and engaging. By simply taking a photo of a tree, users can identify its species using AI, measure its dimensions with augmented reality, and calculate the amount of carbon it captures. The goal is to promote environmental awareness through intuitive and interactive technology.

The development focused on combining accuracy with usability, integrating tools like ARKit for real-world measurements and MapKit for location-based context. One of the main challenges was ensuring reliable calculations from user-captured data, which was addressed through careful calibration and mathematical modeling.
